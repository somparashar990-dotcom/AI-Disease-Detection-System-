Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI model? +
Our machine learning models have been trained on extensive sets of validated medical data across 41 common conditions. While highly accurate for preliminary screening, it must never replace a formal medical diagnosis from a licensed physical examination.
Do I need an account to book? +
No! We believe in frictionless access to healthcare. You can check your symptoms and book an available time slot with a local specialist simply by providing your name and email address.
How do doctors manage appointments? +
Registered doctors get access to a private web portal where they can set their working 1-hour time slots, view incoming bookings, and mark appointments as completed or cancelled.
Is my health data kept private? +
Yes. All predictions and bookings are handled securely. Your symptom data is solely used to generate immediate predictions and pass necessary context to the doctor you choose to book with.
